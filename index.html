trAIn Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

trAIn (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: Your name, birthday, experience level, workout preferences, and app settings.
  • Workout Data: Exercise logs, weights, reps, sets, rest times, and performance history.
  • Authentication Data: Email and user ID via Firebase Authentication.
  • App Usage Data: Device type, app version, and analytics for improving the user experience.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized workout plans and recommendations.
  • To sync your data securely across devices via Firebase Cloud Firestore.
  • To monitor app performance and improve features.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Google Firebase’s infrastructure. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
